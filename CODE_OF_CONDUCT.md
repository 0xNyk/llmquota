# Code of Conduct

## Expected conduct

Be respectful, specific, and focused on the work. Accept technical disagreement without personal
attacks. Do not harass people, expose private information, or post credentials and account data.

This policy applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, and project spaces where a
participant is representing llmquota.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns to the maintainer through the contact options on
[the 0xNyk GitHub profile](https://github.com/0xNyk). Do not open a public issue containing private
details. If the concern involves the maintainer or an urgent platform-safety issue, use GitHub's
reporting tools.

The maintainer may edit or remove harmful content, issue a warning, temporarily restrict
participation, or permanently ban a participant. Decisions consider severity, context, prior
behavior, and risk to affected people. A participant may request one review through the same
contact channel.
